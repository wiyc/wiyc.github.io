The Western Isles Yacht Club is based in Tobermory on the Isle of Mull and our purpose is to promote water-sports. This includes dinghy sailing, coastal rowing, sea swimming, canoeing and, of course, yachting. If you are interested in the sailing cadets club for kids or coastal rowing on Mull get in touch.
