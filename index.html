The purpose of the Western Isles Yacht Club is to promote water-sports. This includes dinghy sailing, coastal rowing, sea swimming, canoeing and, of course, yachting. If you are interested in the sailing cadets club for kids or coastal rowing on Mull get in touch. Taster sessions are available for people interested in these activities
